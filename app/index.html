Our Mission

Our knowledge of import-export operations coupled with strong commercial partnerships and years of experience has given us the power to carefully appraise the mining sector using a value, cost and demand triangle time sequence between active mines and identified reserves. Occasionally, a mine cycle phase may overlap with the subsequent one hence making the process lengthy and high-cost. We are committed to protecting you from exploitation by dishonest producers who choose to exploit innocent customers by passing this cost on in pricing or offer low-grade commodities. While it may seem a quicker and easier way to deal directly with just any producer, lack of market knowledge, limited options, availability and biased advice from some profit-oriented suppliers may compound into high overhead costs and losses for you. At Kayoo Group, our team of professionals is dedicated to offering fast-paced, reliable and customised solutions to your needs. From physically sourcing & procuring commodities to developing a solid export infrastructure to the end consumer. Thanks to the continent’s blessing with abundant natural resources we are fully committed to growing our community and the future of the younger generation.
